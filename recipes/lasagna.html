Lasagna Recipe

yummy lasagna, I haven't made it for a long time

Ingredients

  • meat
  • onion and garlic
  • tomato
  • sugar
  • spices and seasonings
  • lasagna noodles
  • cheese
  • egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
