Chili Recipe

Looks ok, to be honest I've never made chili at home

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can chili beans, not drained
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir ground beef until browned and crumbly.
  3. Stir in kidney beans, chili beans, and tomato sauce.
  4. Bring to a boil and stir in chili powder.
  5. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook, until thickened.
  6. Seasonm to taste with salt and pepper.
