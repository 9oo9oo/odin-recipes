Lasagna Recipe

I'm surprised they have a recipe for just pan frying pork chop lol

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • all-purpose flour, two table spoons
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil

Steps

  1. Season both sides of pork chops with salt and pepper, and sprinkle with flour on both sides.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet.
  3. Add pork chops and pan fry until brown.
  4. When the edges of the chops turn brown, turn and cook the other side until no longer pink.
